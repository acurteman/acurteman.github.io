Cascadia Digital Works, LLC was started in 2017 by Alex Curteman. Alex graduated from Oregon State University with an M.S. in Applied Anthropology as well as a Graduate GIScience Certificate. His thesis was titled "A Morphometric Examination of Lithic Artifacts from the Pilcher Creek Site (35UN147) using GliMR: GIS-based Lithic Morphometric Research".
